Shipping & Delivery Policy

We are committed to delivering orders quickly and safely to ensure a smooth shopping experience for our customers.

1. Shipping Methods:
   - We offer standard shipping, express shipping, and same-day delivery in selected cities.

2. Delivery Time: 
   - Standard shipping: 3-7 business days.  
   - Express shipping: 1-3 business days.  
   - Delivery time may vary depending on customer location and public holidays.

3. Shipping Costs:
   - Costs vary depending on product weight and chosen shipping method.  
   - Free shipping offers are available on orders exceeding a certain amount.

4. Order Tracking:
   - Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number to follow your order until delivery.

5. Delivery Issues: 
   - In case of delayed delivery or lost packages, please contact customer service immediately.  
   - We will resolve the issue promptly with either a reshipment or a refund.
